Uninstalling Process Simulator

  1. To remove Process Simulator click the Windows Start key on your keyboard or on the Windows Taskbar.
  2. Type "Control Panel" and then Enter on your keyboard. The Control Panel should open:

  1. In the Programs section, select Uninstall a program.
  2. Find Process Simulator in the list of programs that appears and then right-click and choose Uninstall.
