Defining Connection Properties

Connections specify routing of entities from one activity to another, or the initial arrival of entities into the process at an activity. To define a connection as a routing, arrival, conveyor (available in material handling editions), or plain connector, right- click on the connection and choose either Routing, Arrival, Conveyor, or Plain Graphic from the context menu. To further define the connection, right-click on the connection and choose Properties from the context menu.
