System Requirements

The minimum and recommended hardware, software, and display requirements for Process Simulator are indicated below.

Hardware and Software

The minimum and recommended hardware and software requirements are:

Minimum Recommended
Pentium 4 - 3.0 GHz Processor Intel Core i7 Processor
1 GB RAM 4 GB RAM
500 MB Available Hard Drive Space 2 GB Available Hard Drive Space
Windows 10 Windows 10

Microsoft Visio 2016 or 2019 (32-bit or 64-bit) must be run once and activated prior to installation of Process Simulator.
