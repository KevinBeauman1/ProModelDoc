Send Routing

The send routing acts as a gate where entities are held at the activity until either a Send statement in logic or an Order triggered from a storage allows the entities to route. For example, bicycle frames route from an activity using a send routing. The frames will accumulate at the activity until a storage further down in the process that stores completed bicycles runs low on frames. At that point, a trigger will occur that orders the bicycle frames to execute their send routing from the activity (i.e. it opens the gate).
