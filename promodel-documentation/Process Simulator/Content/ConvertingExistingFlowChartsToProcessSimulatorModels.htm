Converting Existing Flowcharts to Process Simulator Models

You may convert an existing Visio flowchart to a Process Simulator model by choosing the Convert Diagram option from the Process Simulator ribbon. You can also upgrade old Process Simulator diagrams to the latest version using the Convert Diagram option.

Converting a flowchart will assign simulation properties to the objects in the diagram's layout. Shapes will be assigned as an activity type, and connections will be assigned as a routing type. Simply accept these default types, or change the types with the right-click menu, and choose Simulate to immediately simulate your flowchart.

If a diagram contains simulation properties the Convert Diagram Button will be removed.

Please note: Although you may immediately simulate your flowchart after converting it, you will most likely want to make some modifications to the shapes' properties in order to better define your process.
