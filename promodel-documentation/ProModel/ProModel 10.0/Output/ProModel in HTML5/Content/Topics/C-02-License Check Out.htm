License Check Out

Optionally there are times that you may need to disconnect from the internet. ProModel's licensing now allows for you to Check Out a license to go off line.

To go off line:

1. Go to File > About.

2. Select Checkout in the About screen.

3. The Check Out Window will open allowing you to check your license out for up to 14 days. The license will be checked out once you click the Check Out License button.

4. The next screen will show you the date the license check out will expire.

5. You then will see in the Check In License button in the ribbon. This button allows you to check the license back in prior to the checked out until date.
