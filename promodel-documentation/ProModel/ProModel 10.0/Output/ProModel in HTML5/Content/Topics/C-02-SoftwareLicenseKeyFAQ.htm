Software License Key FAQ

I need to move my software license to another computer, can I do this?

Yes. If the software is installed on that machine, then go ahead and log in. You must be logged out of the previous machine though.

I want to upgrade my computer’s operating system, or make a major change to my computer’s configuration, should I be concerned?

No

My software license no longer appears to be functioning. What should I do?

If the problem persists, please contact ProModel Corporation support at support@promodel.com or call 1-888-PROMODEL.
