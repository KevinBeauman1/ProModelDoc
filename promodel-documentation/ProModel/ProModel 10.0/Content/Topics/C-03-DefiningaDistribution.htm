Defining a Distribution

One way to model whether a patient is either acute or chronic is to sample from a user-defined distribution. To define the distribution, simply click the mouse on the Table button and fill in the distribution parameters. In this exampe, 60% of the patients will receive the value 1 indicating they have an acute condition and 40% will receive a 2 indicating their condition is chronic.

Defining a Distribution

One way to model a part passing or failing an inspection is to sample from a user-defined distribution. To define a user distribution, simply click the mouse on the Table button and fill in the distribution parameters. In this example, 70% of the entities pass inspection and 30% fail.
