AutoCAD®Edition

The ProModel AutoCAD® Edition allows the user to build models within ProModel using Factory Assets and open the assets used in AutoCAD® Architecture or Mechanical 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022 with the use of Autodesk's® Factory Design Utilities 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022.

ProModel AutoCAD® Edition also Provides a Plug in to AutoCAD® Architecture/Mechanical that allows you to give your AutoCAD drawing (DWG) ProModel Simulation Properties. This in turn allows the user to open the DWG in ProModel AutoCAD® Edition, where the model is scaled according to the DWG scale, locations, entities and processing added and the model can start to be simulated and added upon to provide greater detail and complexity.
