Attributes and the Group/Ungroup Statements

Suppose several entities, EntA, EntB, and EntC, are grouped together and called Batch. See Group on page 1 and Ungroup on page 1 for more information. Each of the original entities have attributes with values assigned to them before they are grouped. The Batch is processed for 30 minutes, sent to Loc5 and then ungrouped into the original entities.

The attribute values of the individual entities are not transferred to the grouped entity, Batch. In other words, Att1=0 for the entity, Batch. However, once the entities are ungrouped, they retain their original attribute values. The following diagram graphically shows the concept of grouping.
