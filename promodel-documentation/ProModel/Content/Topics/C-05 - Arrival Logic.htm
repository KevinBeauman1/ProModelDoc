Arrival Logic

Arrival Logic allows you to perform certain logic as an entity enters the system. It is used primarily for assigning initial entity attribute values. Suppose tellers at a bank drive-through process several types of transactions including deposits, withdrawals, and transfers. A user-defined distribution, DT_Service, could be defined representing the probability of a customer requiring a give type of service.
