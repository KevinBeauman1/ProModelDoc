Reports and Graphs

Once your model has been built, and the simulation run, you are ready to begin making important decisions about your real-world process based on your simulation’s data. To help you make the most of the data collected during simulation, MedModelProModelServiceModel comes with powerful, easy-to-use tools that allow you to view your data numerically, in spreadsheet format, or graphically in a variety of charts through the Output Viewer. Or, for Six Sigma analysis select Minitab. Through the Simulation Options dialog, choose between using one or both of these powerful tools.
