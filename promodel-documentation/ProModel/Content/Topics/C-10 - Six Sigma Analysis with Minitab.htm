Six Sigma Analysis with Minitab

MedModel ProModel ServiceModel links and integrates with Minitab to provide users with Six Sigma analysis capability.

 

This integration allows for the tracking of Six Sigma metrics in a model and the automatic reporting of these metrics in Minitab after a scenario is run.

 

Before simulating a scenario in, adjust the Simulation Options dialog to include Minitab to automatically launch at the end of the simulation.
