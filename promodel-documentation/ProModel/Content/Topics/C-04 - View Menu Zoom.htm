Zoom Feature

The Zoom feature allows you to shrink or enlarge the layout by the percentage that you enter.

How to zoom in or out on the layout:

  1. Select Zoom from the View menu.
  2. In the Enter Zoom Percentage text box, type a number for the percentage of the normal magnification of the layout.
  3. Click OK.

Please Note: The minimum and maximum zoom levels are calculated automatically depending on the total size of the layout at 100% zoom.
