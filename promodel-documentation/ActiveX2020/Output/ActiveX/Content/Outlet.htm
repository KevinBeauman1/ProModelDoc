Outlet

The Outlet route is a secondary route and is only used in conjunction with main route types (Percentage, Conditional, Attach, and Send).  Additionally, it is used differently with Percentage and Conditional routes than how it is used with Attach and Send routes.  

  • With Attach and Send routes, an Outlet route is secondary to all Attach or all Send routes from an activity.  
  • With Percentage and Conditional, Outlet routes are defined as secondary to each specific Percentage or Conditional route.  They cannot be defined as general secondary routes to all Percentage or all Conditional routes.
