Sort and Filter

For sorting and filter options, a Sort & Filter button appears to the right of most group headers in the Filter Options panel. These options allow you to control the order in which items are listed, quickly find items in a list, and reduce the list of items if it is too large.

By default, the Sort option is set to "No Sort" which will list any Filter items in the order that they were originally created and appear in your model. To sort the listed items alphabetically, Click "Sort A to Z" or "Sort Z to A."

Click on "Selected Items Only" to reduce the list and display only the items that have their checkbox selected.

To quickly find a specific item in the list, type any part of the name in the Filter... field. As you type, the list will immediately begin to show only those items containing your text.
