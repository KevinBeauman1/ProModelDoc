Introduction to Output Viewer

Once you have defined a process and built a model, you are ready to begin making important decisions based upon the resulting simulation data. To help you make the most of the data collected during your simulation, ProModel applications come with a powerful easy to use Output Viewer. The Output Viewer allows you to view your data numerically in spreadsheet format or graphically through a variety of charts. Output Viewer comes with the following products:

  • Process Simulator
  • ProModel
  • MedModel

When your simulation is completed, you are prompted to view the output results with the following message.

Click Yes to view your simulation results within Output Viewer. If you select Yes, Output Viewer automatically launches and organizes and displays the data gathered during simulation. The default view in Output Viewer provides a Report of four charts and the Scoreboard.
