Location Activity Utilization

Click on LocationActivity Utilization within the Time Series group of the Charts ribbon.

The following chart is generated.

Location Activity Utilization charts show the percentage of a locationan activity's capacity is occupied within given time intervals over the course of a simulation. This allows you to see trends and spikes in activity as the simulation progresses. LocationActivity Utilization charts can only be displayed if the user has selected Time Series as the statistics type for elements in the model. The example above shows the utilization of the Bearing Que from 5 hours to 15 hours. The green and blue columns illustrate the difference in utilization between a scenario with one operator and a scenario with three operators.
