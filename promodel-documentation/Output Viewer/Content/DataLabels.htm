Data Labels

Right-click in any chart and select Data LabelsData labels are lables that further explain or clarify the data in a chart. from the context menu. A submenu will appear. The submenu will vary based upon the chart you are modifying.

Select the chart location where you would like your labels to appear; choices are none, center, inside end, inside base, outside end.
