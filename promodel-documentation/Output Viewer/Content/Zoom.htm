Zoom

Click on Zoom and a magnifying glass appears.

Next, select a zoom style. There are three zoom styles; Scroll, Stripe, or Map.

  • Scroll
  • Stripe
  • Map

Scroll

  1. Click Scroll.
  2. Click and drag the magnifying glass.

  1. Scrollbars appear on the axes lines to navigate.

  1. Click 100% to go back to the full view of the chart.

Stripe

  1. Click Stripe
  2. Click and drag the magnifying glass.

  1. It will only zoom on the Y axis. A preview will appear for further navigation.

  1. Drag the window up or down in the preview to navigate.

  1. Click 100% to go back to the full view of the chart.

Map

  1. Click Map.
  2. Click and drag the magnifying glass.

  1. An interactive map appears which you can use to navigate, for a closer look at your chart.

  1. Move the window around the interactive map to view other areas of the chart.

  1. Click 100% to go back to the full view of the chart.
