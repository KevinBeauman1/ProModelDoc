Entity Failed Arrivals Table

Click on the Tables drop-down and select Failed Arrivals in the Entity group.

A table is generated, which identifies the number of entities that failed to arrive at a specific locationactivity due to insufficient capacity, and contains the following columns of information.

Scenario: The scenario to which the data in the row applies.

Name: The entity name.

Location: Activity: The locationactivity where entities may have failed to arrive.

Total Failed: The total number of entities that failed to arrive at a specific locationactivity due to insufficient capacity during the simulation.
