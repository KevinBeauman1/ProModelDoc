Location Activity State (over time)

Click on LocationActivity State within the Time Series group of the Charts ribbon.

The following chart is generated.

The LocationActivity State time series Gantt chart can be viewed in two ways. When viewing multiple replications, a 100% stacked column Gantt chart is displayed. If viewing as an individual replication, the Gantt chart shows the exact time when the locationactivity changed state and how long it remained in that state. In the multiple replication view, each state is totaled within the time intervals, averaged across replications, and then displayed as a 100% stacked column chart in each interval. The timeline can be panned and zoomed using your mouse scroll wheel.Double-clicking in the chart area will reset the view to show the entire simulation timeline.

% Empty: The percentage of time the locationactivity had no entities.

% In Operation: The percentage of time the locationactivity was processing an entity.

% Blocked: The percentage of time entities spent waiting for a freed destination.

% Waiting: The percentage of time the locationactivity was waiting for a resource, another entity or a wait until condition in order to begin processing or move to the next locationactivity. Any delays in processing move logic (even WAIT statements) are counted as waiting time, including Join, Load,attach actions and Match statements.

% Down: The percentage of time locationactivity was not available due to unscheduled downtime. Multiple capacity locationsactivities only have "Up" and "Down"states which are when the locationactivity is available or unavailable due to a downtime.

Please Note: Changing replications, as well as showing/hiding locationactivity states, are options that can be filtered in the Filter Options panel.
