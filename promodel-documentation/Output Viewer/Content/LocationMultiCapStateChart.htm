Location Activity Multi-Capacity State Chart

Click on LocationActivity Multi Cap within the State group of the Charts ribbon.

The following chart is generated.

This chart shows a horizontal stacked bar chart summarizing the states of each multi capacity locationactivity during the simulation run. States describe the condition of these objects.

% Empty: The percentage of time a locationan activity had no entities.

% Partially Occupied: The percentage of time a locationan activity had entities, but was not filled to capacity.

% Full: The percentage of time a locationan activity was filled to capacity with entities.
