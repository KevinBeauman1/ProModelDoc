States or Conditions

States or Conditions Defined

The simulation tracks the following conditions:

 

% In Use: The percentage of time the resource spent servicing a locationan activity or other resource that was down. This also includes downtime.
% Travel To Park: The percentage of time the resource spent traveling to a path node to park or traveling to its downtime node.
% Travel to Use: The percentage of time the resource spent traveling to a location or other resource to transport or process an entity, or to service a location or other resource. This includes pick-up time.
% In Move Logic: The percentage of time the item was moving from one locationactivity to another during the simulation, including any delay time incurred in move logic, and including any time waiting for a resource to become available when that resource is requested in the move logic.
% Waiting: The percentage of time the locationactivity was waiting for a resource, another entity or a wait until condition in order to begin processing or move to the next locationactivity. Any delays in processing move logic (even WAIT statements) are counted as waiting time, including Join, Load,attach actions and Match statements.
% In Operation: The percentage of time the locationactivity was actually processing an entity.
% Blocked: The percentage of time entities spent waiting for a freed destination.
% Empty: The percentage of time the locationactivity had no entities.
% Partially Occupied: The percentage of time the locationactivity has entities, but is not filled to capacity.
% Full: The percentage of time the locationactivity was filled to capacity with entities.
% Setup: The percentage of time an operation was being set up during the simulation.
% Idle: The percentage of time a resource was available, but not in use.
% Down: The percentage of time a resource was not available due to unscheduled downtime.
