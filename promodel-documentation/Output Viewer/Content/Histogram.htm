Histogram

Click on Histogram within the Time Series group in the Charts ribbon.

The following chart is generated.

A histogram is a bar chart that shows the percentage of time that time series data fell within a particular range of values. The Histogram: Bearing Que Contents example shows what percentage of time the contents of the Bearing Que was below 8, 16, 24, etc. You can see how having two and three operators reduces the amount of entities in the queue to less than 18.
