Location Activity Multi-Capacity State Pie Chart

Select LocationActivity Multi Cap from the Pie Charts drop-down within the State group of the Charts ribbon.

The following pie chart is generated.

This pie chart displays each state as a separate section of the pie based upon the percentage of overall simulation time the multi capacity locationactivity spent in a state.

% Empty: The percentage of time the locationactivity had no entities occupying its capacity.

% Partially Occupied: The percentage of time the locationactivity had entities, but was not filled to capacity.

% Full: The percentage of time the locationactivity was filled to capacity with entities.
