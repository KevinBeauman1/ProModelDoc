Location Activity Multi-Capacity States Table

Click on the Tables drop-down and select Multi Cap States in the LocationActivity group.

A table is generated, which lists the percentage of time multi-capacity locationsactivities spent in various states, and contains the following columns of information.

Scenario: The scenario to which the data in the row applies.

Name: The locationactivity name.

Scheduled Time: The total amount of time the locationactivity was scheduled to be available. (Excludes off-shift time, break time, and scheduled downtimes.)

% Empty: The percentage of time the locationactivity had no entities.

% Part Occupied: The percentage of time the locationactivity has entities, but was not filled to capacity (100% of time - %Full - %Empty).

% Full: The percentage of time the locationactivity was full to capacity with entities.

% Down: The percentage of time the locationactivity was down as the result of unscheduled downtimes. This does not exclude the possibility of overlap with any of the previous three states
