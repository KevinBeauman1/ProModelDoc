Percentage

  • Percent  The percentage of time this route is selected. If multiple Percentage routes originate from the same activity, and the sum of their percents does not equal 100 percent, an error will be displayed on the connection lines indicating that the sums do not equal 100.
  • New Name   If you wish to have your entity renamed during routing, you may enter a new name to replace the old one.
