Outlet

The Outlet route is a secondary route and is only used in conjunction with main route types.  

  • With Percentage and Conditional, Outlet routes are defined as secondary to each specific Percentage or Conditional route.  They cannot be defined as general secondary routes to all Percentage or all Conditional routes.

In the following example, Documents arrive at Activity 1 and then route from Activity 1 to Activity 2 on a 100-percentage routing type. However, when Activity 2 goes off-shift, Documents will take the Outlet route, which goes to Activity 3.
