Viewing Output Results

Once your process has been defined, and then simulated, you are ready to begin making important decisions about your real-world process based on your simulation’s data. To help you make the most of the data collected during simulation, Process Simulator comes with the powerful, easy-to-use Output Viewer. The Output viewer allows you to view your data numerically, in spreadsheet format, or graphically in a variety of charts.

When your simulation completes, you will be prompted to view the output statistics. If you choose to view the statistics, Output Viewer will launch and display a scoreboard of some of the results. If you wish to view additional charts and reports you may do so while in Output Viewer.  For more information on Output Viewer, launch the help system from within the program.

Output results are stored in an .rdb file, which has the same name as your Visio document (e.g. MyDrawing.rdb) and is saved in the same directory as your Visio document.
