Output Viewer

Once you have defined a process and built a model, you are ready to begin making important decisions based upon the resulting simulation data. To help you make the most of the data collected during your simulation, Process Simulator comes with a powerful easy to use Output Viewer. The Output Viewer allows you to view your data numerically in spreadsheet format or graphically in a variety of charts.

To view results in Output Viewer, click the Output Viewer button in the Process Simulator ribbon or click Yes to view results at the end of the simulation run.

Please note: If a simulation or scenarios have previously run, Output Viewer will automatically load the simulated results when it opens.

When you run a simulation and then click Yes to view the results in Output Viewer, it launches the program and displays the data gathered during simulation. The first view in Output Viewer is a predefined set of five charts called the Scoreboard report.

Scoreboard

When your simulation ends, and you choose to view the statistics, Output Viewer launches with a Scoreboard report view by default. The Scoreboard report contains charts that display the following information:

  • Entity State Chart   Shows a stacked bar chart of the entities in the model and the percentage of time they spent in different states.
  • Scoreboard   Provides key statistics for the entities in your model.
  • Resource State Chart   Shows a stacked bar chart of the resources in the model and the percentage of time they spent in different states.
  • Activity State Charts   Two charts show stacked bar charts of the activities (single and multi-capacity) in the model and the percentage of time they spent in different states.

You are not limited to the reports and charts displayed in the Scoreboard report, and may open others while in Output Viewer.

Output Viewer Help

For detailed information and assistance while working within Output Viewer, click the Help icon located in the upper right hand corner of the Output Viewer application.
