System Requirements

The following sections indicate the minimum hardware, software, and display requirements for 3D Animator.

Hardware and Software

The minimum hardware and software requirements are:

Minimum

Pentium 4 - 3.0 GHz

1 GB RAM

2 GB Available Hard Drive Space

Windows 7,Vista, XP

64 MB Video RAM

Either Nvidia GeForce 2 or better, or ATI Rage Fury or better

DirectX 9.0c

Other Requirements and Recommendations

Other requirements and recommendations include:

  • Adobe Acrobat Reader, Version 5.0 or later to read the .pdf files installed with 3D Animator.
  • Graphics set to 16 bits color (high color) or greater.
  • Latest video card drivers. Please check with your video card manufacturer for driver updates.
