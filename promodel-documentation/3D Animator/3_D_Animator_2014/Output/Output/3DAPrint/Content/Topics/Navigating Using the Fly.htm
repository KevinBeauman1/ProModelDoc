Navigating Using the Fly-Through Technique

The fly-through technique is a simple way to move the camera as if it were free floating through the layout.

 

To do so click and hold the right mouse button over the layout, and use the E, S, D, and F keys on the keyboard to move the camera.

 

The E and D keys will move the camera forward and backward, while the S and F keys will pan the camera laterally. Moving the mouse while holding the right mouse button will rotate the camera.

 

This keyboard and mouse combination allows you to fly the camera through the layout.
